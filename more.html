UKAid has team in 34 countries. Together with the help of our many partners and the help of the public people we transform millions of lives for the better every year.

UKAid is a well-known and established charity as its staggering numbers have proved them to be well established. Since UKAid 1981 WaterAid has reached 24.9 million people with clean water.

We are UKAid Our vision is a world where everyone, everywhere has safe water, clean toielts and hygiene.

Our mission is to transform the lives of the less fortunate people who don’t have clean water by improving access to safe water for them and giving them good toilets and hygiene levels.

Our values define our culture and unite us across the many countries in which we work. They are at the very heart of UKAid – who we are, what we do and how we do it.
