UKAid Strategy


Extreme poverty won't end until we all come together and help everyone, everywhere to have clean water, decent toilets and good hygiene. What's at the heart of our strategy? Making water, toilets and hygiene normal for everyone, everywhere as these are basic human rights.

At UKAid were determined make that a reality within a generation and we as a team believe that the best way to make this possible is by working closely with others.
UKAid is part of an active global network who works closely with campaigners, fundraisers, engineers and advisors.
